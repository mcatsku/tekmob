About this project by Atte

This is a poster-inspired, browser-based calculator. It supports arithmetic, parentheses, and functions like sqrt, sin, cos, tan, log, and ln. Designed for simplicity and clarity, with bold colors and handwritten accents.

Touch! → Calculate • Mail! → Share

How to use

  • Type an expression and press Calculate or Enter.
  • Use functions like sqrt(16), sin(pi/2).
  • History keeps your recent results.
